Research Summary

I am a highly passionate researcher specializing in the cybersecurity of smart grids, with a strong focus on addressing the challenges posed by the decentralization of power systems driven by the increasing integration of Distributed Energy Resources (DERs). My work leverages cutting-edge technologies to develop innovative solutions that enhance the resilience and security of cyber-physical energy infrastructures. I am committed to advancing the future of energy consumption towards more intelligent, decentralized, and cyber-resilient systems.
